Instructions

  • Press 'space' to start and restart the game.
  • The white platforms are your key to survival!
  • Use the arrow keys (left, right, up) and space to bounce the black box (The Action Box) safely between platforms.
  • Try to get the highest score you can without touching the ground.
  • Powerups
    • Powerups are coloured boxes that fall from the sky that provide special abilities.
    • Activate a powerup by moving the action box over it.
    • A powerup will activate for a random amount of time and they will vary in power.
    • More valuable powerups are smaller and therefore more difficult to attain.
    • Blue powerups will lower gravity, making it easier to glide between platforms.
    • Red powerups will add a points multiplier
